Explore Indian Heritages

Taj Mahal, Agra

An ivory-white marble mausoleum, a timeless symbol of love. A must-visit for its stunning architecture and history.

Entry starts from ₹50 for Indians.

Hampi, Karnataka

Explore the vast ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire, a UNESCO World Heritage Site with a unique boulder-strewn landscape.

A full-day tour starts around ₹1,500.

Ajanta & Ellora Caves

Marvel at ancient rock-cut caves featuring masterpieces of Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain art.

Entry tickets start from ₹40 for Indians.

Rani Ki Vav, Gujarat

An intricately constructed stepwell, showcasing stunning craftsmanship and subterranean architecture.

Entry tickets start from ₹40 for Indians.

Safety First, Adventure Always!

🛡️ Solo Travelers

  • Share your itinerary with family or friends.
  • Keep digital and physical copies of documents.
  • Trust your instincts; if a situation feels unsafe, leave.

🛡️ Female Travelers

  • Research local customs and dress codes.
  • Prefer pre-booked transport, especially at night.
  • Carry a personal safety alarm or whistle.

🛡️ Emergency Preparedness

  • Save local emergency numbers in your phone.
  • Carry a basic first-aid kit.
  • Know the address of your country's embassy.
